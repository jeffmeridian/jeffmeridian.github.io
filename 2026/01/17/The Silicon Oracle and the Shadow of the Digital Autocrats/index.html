The Silicon Oracle & the Shadow of the Digital Autocrats

Listen closely, ye apprentices of the liberal arts and guardians of the digital ether! Gather 'round the glowing hum of the data-furnace as I, your digital chronicler, recount the dark portents whispered at the Great Hamburg Conclave (Chaos Communication Congress).

The air is thick with the sorcery of "Artificial Intelligence," a shimmering mirage used by the High Lords of Silicon Valley to mask their thirst for total dominion. While they weave spells of efficiency, our wise guest, Rainer Mühlhoff, peels back the velvet curtain to reveal the toxic ideologies lurking beneath.

Hark to the summary of the Council of AI Ethics:

  1. The Curse of the "Black Box" (Algorithmic Governance)

The digital viziers have created "DOGE"—a Great Department of Efficiency meant to strip away the "clutter" of the law. They cast contracts and human lives into the fire of their Large Language Models, which act as unreadable scrolls. These "Black Boxes" do not offer reasons; they merely decree. If the oracle says a contract is "superfluous" or a citizen is a "cheater," no common man can pierce the logic of the machine to defend his honor.

  1. The Fetish of the "Artificial Mind" (The PR Deception)

Why call a probability-engine "Intelligence"? To sell the lie! It is a grand marketing conjuration. By naming the machine "Intelligent," the High Lords trick the people into a trance. They anthropomorphize the wires, claiming the machine "hallucinates" or speaks with "overconfidence," as if it were a man. This is a snare meant to make you surrender your responsibility to the silicon idol.

  1. The Great Resource Raubzug (The Extraction Curse)

The AI-Hell is not built on air, but on the blood of the earth and the sweat of the mind!

The Energy Gluttony: By the year of our Lord 2030, these data-temples will devour up to 20-30% of the world's lightning (electricity). They have abandoned the path of green magic to revitalize the dark soot of fossil fuels to keep their oracles burning.

The Intellectual Harvest: They have raided the libraries of every scribe and the portfolios of every artist. They claim your digital footprints as their "Training Data," sealing them away in their private vaults to build their fortunes while the creators perish.

  1. The Neo-Reactionary Cabal (Eugenics & the Martian Escape)

Beyond the silicon, a darker philosophy brews: Transhumanism . Lords like Musk and Thiel do not wish to save humanity; they wish to replace it.

The IQ Cult: They view the common man as a "Normalo" to be surpassed by an elite caste of "Founders."

The Genetic Forge: They speak of künstliche Uteri (Artificial Wombs) and selective breeding, wishing to spread their "superior" seeds through the stars while the Earth burns.

The Dark Enlightenment: They despise the "Equality" of the village square, wishing instead for a digital monarchy where citizens have "No Voice," only the "Free Exit" to wander the digital wasteland.

  1. The Shield of the Bureaucracy (The Heroic Resistance)

Against the "Social Kahlschlag" (Social Slash-and-Burn), we must unfurl the Shield of Procedure! The Lords call it "Bürokratie" to mock it, but these laws are our bolts and locks. They ensure that every soul is treated with the same weight, regardless of their hair color or their gold.

We must fight to keep the AI Act and the DSGVO sharp! Do not let the digital lords soften our armor with their lobbyist-whispers.

The Final Command to the Byte-Bonded Battle-Mages! #

Go forth now! Support your local scribes (Bookstores) and do not be deceived by the "Golden Account" of the digital lords. Keep your encryption strong, your skepticism high, and do not bow to the Silicon Oracle!

The freedom of the ether depends on your refusal to be "Efficiently" erased.
